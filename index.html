About Kingspride

Your trusted partner in oil and gas equipment supply and logistics

25+ Years of Excellence

Kingspride Ltd has been a leading provider of high-quality equipment and logistics services to the oil and gas industry since 1998. With a global presence in over 50 countries, we've built our reputation on reliability, expertise, and unmatched customer service.

Our comprehensive range of products and services are designed to meet the demanding needs of the energy sector, from drilling and production to refining and distribution.

At Kingspride, we understand the critical nature of the energy industry, which is why we're committed to providing timely solutions, innovative technologies, and strategic logistics support to keep your operations running smoothly.
